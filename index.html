I’m Melissa.

A couple of years ago, when I was just out of high school and I had no idea what I wanted to do in life (as probably alot of us), I started studying communication.

After I graduated I started working at various media companies and premium publishers. I am currently working as an account manager at Taboola, a content discovery platform.

Now, after a few years and with a lot of curiosity - not to say fear - and craving for a project that was 100% mine, I decided to do a coding bootacamp. I want to tell you that it was strategic and a grand plan of mine, but it was a happy accident that turned into something kinda life-changing.

I started to learn coding as a fun-hobby-kinda-side-job, and I quickly realized it has become a way to connect with people all around the world.



I am a Cuban living in Mexico City. I’m continuously looking for new things to learn and I'm always improvising calculatedly.
